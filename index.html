Our Services

Packaging For Coffee

Our machines can dose coffee blends such as ground coffee, 2 in 1, 3 in 1, 4 in 1 mixes, cappuccino, coffee beans, ginseng, decaffeinated ground coffee, ground barley, soluble granular coffee, soluble powder barley, soluble powder coffee.

Packaging For Sugar

We design our solution for free-flowing products such as brown sugar, creamer, fructose, white sugar, stevia, sweetener. This solution guarantees high-speed packaging standards

Packaging For Drinks

We design our solutions for powders and granules such as soluble chamomile, soluble tea, drink and chocolate powders. On the other hand, gravimetric and piston pump dosers are the suitable options for liquid products such as alcoholic drinks.

Packaging For Spices

We design our solutions for seasonings such as: chili flakes, cloves, crushed spice mixes, curry, dried chopped herbs and vegetables, dry seasonings (with particles), glutamate, granulated soups or soup powders, ground pepper, nutmeg, peppercorns, saffron, salt, spice mix.

Our Machines

Our machines and lines are specifically dedicated to single-dose packages like stick packs and sachets. For example, we can dose from 0.1 up to 100 gr of coffee and derivatives in a single pack, whose dimensions vary depending on your marketing goals, nature of the product and production requirements. For complete lines for cartoning, our systems allow you to put from 1 up to 100 or more sachets or stickpacks in the same carton. We have developed patented counting units for any amount of packages to be picked and put into cartons. In recent years, our philosophy is to save as much material and product as possible, both for economic and environmental sustainability.
